Linked Open Data

Project overview

La Dolce Vita- Link Open Data has been developed for the examination of 'Knowledge Organization and Digital Methods in the Cultural Heritage Domain' course of DHDK, University of Bologna, a.y. 2018/2019. On the occasion of the sixtieth anniversary of the release of the movie "La Dolce Vita", we decided to implement a Link Open Data model around and focus on the production and distribution of this cult movie directed by Federico Fellini in 1960.

Workflow

  • Items

    We have made a selection of 10 items of different types coming from archives, museums, libraries websites, and they represent several aspects of the film "La Dolce Vita".

  • E/R model

    An entity/relation model that describes our idea and highlights the relationship between entities.

  • Metadata analysis

    Analysis of the metadata standards used by the various institutions from which we have selected our items.

  • Metadata alignement>

    Alignment between the different standards previously selected to describe metadata related to people, places, dates and concepts.

  • Theoretical model

    Theoretical bases on our idea: the theoretical model is mainly based on the E/R model, but it extends it with new properties to properly represent the scenario.

  • Conceptual model

    Creation of conceptual model by reusing existing standard consider the most appropriate once which allow to represent the terms we need for describing the scenario.

  • Description of data

    Description of data on the base of the previous conceptual model

  • RDF

    Description of our data in rdf language (Turtle and XML) and a graphical representation of them.

  • Connection of data

    Our data connected to other related items through URIs. Related because of the sharing of one of the features of the object.
